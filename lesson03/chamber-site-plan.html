Site Plan - Samanta Baez

"Quebec Chamber of Commerce"

This name was selected because it is a representation of the city for which the chamber will be based on. It is the city in which I currently live in.

Purpose

The purposes for this website will be to promote tourism in the city, and to also promote local businesses and organizations. This website will be a way to strengthen the city's reputaion; giving the citizens (and tourists) information, announcements, and events happening their city.

Scenarios

  • Will any events be taking place in the near future?
  • How can I join/make part of the business chamber?
  • What business firms or organizations are associated with this chamber of commerce?
  • Where are the business associated located, and how do I contact them?
