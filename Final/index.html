About Us

Welcome to Scoots, your premier motor scooter rental company located on the vibrant island of Cozumel, nestled in the western Caribbean Sea just off the coast of Mexico.

At Scoots, we pride ourselves on being more than just a rental service – we're your trusted partner in exploring the beauty of Cozumel. As a locally owned and operated business, we are deeply rooted in the community and dedicated to providing exceptional service to both our neighbors and visitors alike.
