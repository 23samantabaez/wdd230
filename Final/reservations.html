Reservation and rental agreements

  • Vehicle Use: The rented vehicle is to be used solely for personal transportation purposes. Any commercial use of the vehicle is strictly prohibited.
  • Driver Requirements: Only drivers listed on the rental agreement are authorized to operate the vehicle. All drivers must possess a valid driver's license and meet the minimum age requirement specified by the rental company.
  • Vehicle Inspection: The renter is responsible for inspecting the vehicle for any pre-existing damages or issues before taking possession. Any damages should be reported to the rental company immediately to avoid liability.
  • Fuel Policy: The vehicle should be returned with the same level of fuel as when it was rented. Failure to do so may result in additional charges.
  • Insurance Coverage: The rental agreement includes basic insurance coverage for the rented vehicle. Additional insurance options are available for purchase to provide further coverage.
  • Late Return: The renter is expected to return the vehicle by the specified return date and time. Late returns may result in additional charges.
  • Prohibited Activities: Smoking, off-road driving, and towing other vehicles are strictly prohibited. Violation of these terms may result in penalties and additional charges.
  • Responsibility for Damages: The renter is responsible for any damages to the vehicle, including but not limited to accidents, collisions, and theft. The rental company may charge the renter for repair costs and loss of use.
  • Additional Charges: The rental agreement may include additional charges for services such as additional drivers, child seats, and navigation systems.
