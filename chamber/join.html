Membership Benefits

NP Membership (Non-Profit Organizations, No Fee):

  • Access to educational resources and webinars on non-profit management.
  • Networking opportunities with other non-profit leaders and organizations.
  • Discounted or free admission to non-profit-focused events and conferences.
  • Listing in the organization's directory of non-profit partners.
  • Access to grant opportunities and fundraising resources.

Bronze Membership:

  • Access to monthly networking events and mixers.
  • Discounted rates on advertising in the organization's newsletter or website.
  • Invitation to an annual members-only conference or workshop.
  • Recognition on the organization's website as a Bronze-level member.
  • Access to a members-only online forum or discussion group.

Silver Membership:

  • All benefits of Bronze Membership, plus:
  • Increased discount on advertising rates.
  • Priority registration and discounted rates for training programs and workshops.
  • Access to exclusive webinars or seminars with industry experts.
  • Recognition in the organization's annual report as a Silver-level member.

Gold Membership:

  • All benefits of Silver Membership, plus:
  • Complimentary registration for all organization events.
  • Exclusive invitation to a yearly VIP reception with industry leaders.
  • Featured spotlight on the organization's website or newsletter.
  • Priority access to sponsorship opportunities for organization events.
